### Main Features
**🤖 AI Sermon Generator**
- Create sermons in 6 preaching styles (Pastoral, Evangelistic, Prophetic, Youth, Teaching, Encouragement)
- Choose from 5 theological traditions (General, Protestant, Evangelical, Pentecostal, Orthodox)
- Generate in English, Afaan Oromoo, or Amharic
- Audio narration with customizable speed (0.75x to 1.25x)
- Export as PDF for printing or sharing

**📖 AI Bible Companion**
- Ask Scripture questions and get biblical answers
- Multilingual support with natural, native responses
- Daily verse of the day with audio playback
- Learn and grow through AI-guided Bible study

**🙏 Prayer Journal & Tools**
- Create and track prayer requests
- Mark prayers as answered
- Set prayer reminders
- Community prayer board to pray for others
- Share prayers and support fellow believers

**📚 Offline Bible & Audio**
- Read the Bible offline without internet
- Save favorite verses and notes
- Listen to audio Bible chapters anywhere
- Built-in verse highlighting and bookmarks

**✨ Smart Bible Features**
- Verse sharing with beautiful images
- Daily devotional readings
- Reading streaks & spiritual growth tracking
- Prayer reminders at custom times

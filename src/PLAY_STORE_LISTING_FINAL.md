## Full Description (4000 chars)

FaithLight is your all-in-one Bible companion designed to help you grow spiritually every day.

Whether you are a pastor, church leader, or believer, FaithLight gives you powerful tools to study the Bible, prepare sermons, and deepen your relationship with God.

### ✨ Key Features:

**📖 Bible Reader**
Read the Bible anytime with a clean and easy experience.

**🤖 AI Sermon Generator**
Create powerful sermons in seconds in your language.
Perfect for pastors, leaders, and personal study.

**🙏 Prayer Journal**
Write and track your prayers with a private and organized journal.

**🎧 Audio Bible**
Listen to Bible chapters and download them for offline use.

**🌍 Multilingual Support**
Use FaithLight in English, Afaan Oromoo, Amharic, and more.

**📅 Daily Verse**
Receive daily inspiration with Bible verses and reflections.

**🧠 AI Bible Guide**
Ask questions and get clear explanations of Bible passages.

**📤 Share Verses**
Create beautiful verse images and share with friends and family.

### 💡 Why FaithLight?

- Designed for real spiritual growth
- Supports multiple languages
- Works offline
- Built for both individuals and church leaders

**Grow your faith. Share the Word. Stay connected with God.**

Download FaithLight today.
